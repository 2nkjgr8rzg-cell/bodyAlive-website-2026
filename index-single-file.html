FAQ

What is Advanced Function | Advanced Therapy?

Its BodyAlives original method: skilled bodywork combined with assisted movement. Its designed to support comfort, mobility, and usable function. You stay in charge. We assist you with handson work, structure, and clear communication.

How long are sessions?

Minimum 90 minutes. You can choose longer sessions in 30minute steps (120 / 150 / 180+ minutes). Longer sessions give us time to work properly and leave you feeling finished, not rushed.

Is there a 90minute option for Advanced Therapy?

Yes. The 90minute Advanced Therapy session is a trial option so you can test whether it fits. For deeper change, most people choose longer sessions or structured intensives.

Do you offer packages?

Yes. We offer 3 / 6 / 9session options. As a guide: 3 sessions are often done across one week, 6 across two weeks, and 9 across three consecutive weeks. The right fit depends on your goal and schedule.

How do I book and whats the best way to contact you?

Booking is online. For questions or help choosing the right service/length, text is best: 0467 467 222. Email is available for followup details once somethings organised: BodyAlivemassagenow@gmail.com.

Do you provide health-fund rebates?

We focus on handson outcomes and highstandard sessions rather than rebate processing. If youre after results rather than paperwork, youll feel at home here.

Pregnancy massage?

We can offer massage during the second trimester with clearance from your doctor or midwife. We dont offer massage during the first trimester. Third trimester may be considered casebycase.

Do you diagnose or treat medical conditions?

No diagnosis. This is massage therapy and bodywork in a professional clinic setting. If something feels medically urgent or youre unsure what youre dealing with, its wise to check in with an appropriate registered professional.
