Lifestyle inputs

Food, habits, and the quiet inputs that keep dragging you backward

Hands-on work helps. Results hold better when your daily inputs support recovery instead of fighting it.

What we can cover (as part of some programs)

  • Identify common foods and products that may contribute to inflammation and poor recovery
  • Make simple swaps that support steadier energy and better repair
  • Build habits that make progress easier to maintain
Not fads: this is practical education and habit support. For medical nutrition needs, allergies or diagnosed conditions, work with your GP or a qualified dietitian.
Why it matters: if the baseline inputs are inflammatory, the body keeps re-tightening and re-guarding. Better inputs make results easier to hold.
No lectures: the goal is a cleaner, more workable baseline you can actually stick to.
