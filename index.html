Short session

Emergency Discomfort Relief (30 minutes)

Rapid relief for acute musculoskeletal discomfort. This is not Advanced Therapy. It’s a short, targeted session to reduce pain and restore basic movement when you’re stuck.

Suggested approach (what the 30 minutes is for)

Fast triage + targeted decompression + a simple plan so the relief holds.

1) Quick screen
Confirm it’s musculoskeletal and safe to treat.
2) Decompress
Reduce load and calm the hot spot so movement stops fighting you.
3) Reset pattern
Restore basic joint glide and coordination in the area.
4) Protect the change
1–2 simple movements or positions so you don’t rebound by tonight.
  • Best for: acute strain, spasm, sudden joint restriction, painful movement patterns.
  • Not for: unexplained severe pain, internal/systemic/organ pain, neurological red flags.
  • Next step: if you want the full rebuild, book Advance Function | Advanced Therapy (90+ minutes).
