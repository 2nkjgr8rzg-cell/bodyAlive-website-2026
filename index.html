FAQ

Straight answers for people who take their body seriously.

What is the difference between Spa Massage and Advanced Therapy?
Spa Massage is for relaxation and nervous system reset: long-form, slow, and designed for deep unwind. Advanced Therapy is for targeted outcomes in pain, mobility, function, and performance. Both start at 90 minutes, but the intent and structure of the session are different.
Why is the minimum 90 minutes?
Short sessions often mean rushing and partial work. BodyAlive runs on 90-minute minimums so there is enough time to either properly unwind (Spa) or properly assess, treat, and plan (Advanced Therapy). It respects your time and the work being done.
Do you offer health fund rebates?
No. Health fund rebates are not provided. The focus here is on session quality, time, and outcomes for health seekers, not on fitting into rebate categories.
How do I book?
All bookings are handled online through the Square booking portal. You can view availability in real time, choose your service type, and secure your session with card details.
What if I want a spa session but everything is booked out?
Spa Massage and Facial Spa + Massage are currently fully booked until 8 Jan 2026. You can check the booking portal for cancellations or new availability as that date gets closer. Advanced Therapy remains available for those who need targeted work sooner.
What is your cancellation policy?
Sessions reserve a dedicated 90-minute block. Changes and cancellations must be made within the time frame specified in the booking system. Late changes or missed sessions may attract a fee to keep the schedule fair for other health seekers.
