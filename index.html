FAQ

The usual questions, answered once so nobody has to retype them.

Do you provide health-fund rebates?

We dont process or provide health-fund rebates. Treatments focus on restoring function, improving mobility, and easing pain with real therapeutic methods rather than paperwork.

Can the facial spa be done without products?

Yes. Product-free is available on request. If you do want products, you can mix and match: cleanse, scrub, mask, or any combination.

How do I see whats available?

Use the online booking portal. It shows live availability and the current service menu.

What should I book if Im unsure?

If you want relaxation and a premium spa feel, book Spa Massage. If you want structured, outcome-focused work, book Advance Function | Advanced Therapy.
