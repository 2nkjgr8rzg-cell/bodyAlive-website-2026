Emergency Discomfort Relief (30 minutes)

Rapid relief for acute musculoskeletal discomfort. This is not Advanced Therapy. It’s a short, targeted session to reduce pain and restore basic movement when you’re stuck.

Book Emergency Relief

Suggested approach (what the 30 minutes is for)

Fast triage + targeted decompression + a simple plan so the relief holds.

  1. Quick screen: confirm it’s musculoskeletal and safe to treat.
  2. Decompress: reduce load and calm the hot spot so movement stops fighting you.
  3. Reset pattern: restore basic joint glide and coordination in the area.
  4. Protect the change: 1–2 simple movements or positions so you don’t rebound by tonight.
  • Best for: acute strain, spasm, sudden joint restriction, painful movement patterns.
  • Not for: unexplained severe pain, internal/systemic/organ pain, neurological red flags.
  • Next step: if you want the full rebuild, book Advance Function | Advanced Therapy (2 hours).
Safety: If pain is unexplained, severe, worsening, or linked with fever, chest pain, new numbness/weakness, loss of bladder/bowel control, or major trauma, medical assessment comes first.
